Our Mission in 2022

Mission

We are on the mission to build a large community that wants to grow together. A group of people who are eager enough to learn. And give enough time of their precious life, to lift them up from their current situation to a better one.

Vission

In the next 30 years, lots of our traditional jobs will be obsolete. Lots of new jobs will also be created. The newly formed jobs will be tech-savvy. We see a world, where people in all spares of society are sound technologically.

Goals

People who want to be successful in the new scenario must train them up today. In 2022 we want to make a community of at least 10 thousand people. We will scale up their capacity and divide them into different subgroups. Lastly we will send them in different training programs.
